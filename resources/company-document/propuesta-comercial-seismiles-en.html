Who We Are

My name is Pablo Fortunato, CEO of The Madness Expeditions, and together with a group of friends, we founded the company in 2005. Our Logistics Base is located in Mendoza, at the foot of the Andes Mountains. Our Operations Base is in Buenos Aires, the capital of Argentina, from where we have most of our communication access to the rest of the country.

Since then, we have started this company organizing and guiding groups throughout Argentina, in the Andes Mountains from North to South! Over time, we began organizing expeditions to The Seismiles volcanoes & peaks, starting in 2012. Over the years, we have developed and improved our services in this area, driven by experience and a desire for growth. Our experience working on Aconcagua, an international market, has given us insight into what clients need for comfort, safety, and the certainty of reaching the summits. We apply all of this knowledge to this region to ensure a truly exceptional experience. Since this area is so "pristine" and the public often seeks these unique experiences, we've focused our expertise on this region.

We have experience in Bolivia, Peru, and Ecuador, as well as international expeditions on other continents. Since our inception, we have grown and gained experience in high-altitude mountaineering, providing service, logistics, safety, and quality for each expedition and experience.

The Destination

Our specialty is the region known as "The SixThousanders" — where the world's highest volcanoes are located, in the provinces of Salta, Catamarca, La Rioja and San Juan (Mercedario). A unique, little-known and underexplored area with enormous potential for specialized tourism. Each volcano has its own charm, history, culture, beauty, difficulty and logistics. In a single expedition, 1, 2 or even 3 summits can be combined.

Many of these are "Wild Style" expeditions — requiring mobile base camps, while others make use of mountain lodges and refuges available in the area. We have the equipment, capacity, logistics and safety standards to carry them out. We have our own personal gear and on-site logistics to ensure each outing is of the highest quality. Ideal season: November to mid-March · Ideal as pre-acclimatization for other SixThousanders expeditions or Himalayan ascents.

The "SixThousanders" are located in the Argentine Puna region, in the northwestern provinces — La Rioja, Catamarca and Salta —, forming the largest concentration of volcanoes over six thousand meters in South America. The landscape is dry and uniquely beautiful: a wide variety of birds, lagoons, hot springs, wild and untouched flora and fauna. In addition to the main summit, each expedition includes intermediate acclimatization ascents that are extraordinary experiences in their own right.
